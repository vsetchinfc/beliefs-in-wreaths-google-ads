- "Stylish christmas wreaths" started tight, now casting wider net

- Negative keywords must be added continuously, not just at setup

- "Luxury christmas decor" invisible at $6.00 for 3 straight days
- In competitive luxury auction, need $7+ bids to participate
- Quality Score alone can't overcome insufficient bid
- Secondary keywords need equal or higher bids to compete with primary

- 16 ‚Üí 44 impressions = 175% growth

- Engagement rate is early warning signal for waste
