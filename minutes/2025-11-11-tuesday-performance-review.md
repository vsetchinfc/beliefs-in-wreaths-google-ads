## Key Learnings

### 1. Attribution Lag is Real
- Sales happen in real-time (Shopify)
- Google Ads shows conversions 2-24 hours later
- Always check Shopify for actual sales, not just Google Ads dashboard

### 2. Multi-Session Journeys Common
- Nov 9 ad click ‚Üí Nov 11 purchase
- Customers need time to consider luxury purchases ($285)
- First-click attribution matters for high-value items

### 3. Phrase Match = Double-Edged Sword
- Generates impressions and clicks
- But 77.5% waste from wrong variations
- Consider exact match keywords for tighter control

### 4. Budget Constraints Affect Keyword Serving
- 86.96% lost impression share to budget
- Hero keyword stopped serving after 2 PM
- Higher budget doesn't always = more sales if waste is high

### 5. Mobile is King
- 100% of conversions from mobile devices
- Desktop/tablet waste must be minimized
- Mobile bid adjustment (+20%) justified
